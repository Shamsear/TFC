### New Match Scenarios 💡

#### 1. **Comeback Victory** 🔥
- Trigger: Team was losing at halftime but won
- Example: "Down 0-2 at HT, Liverpool storms back to win 3-2!"
- Context: Check if team was behind at some point

#### 2. **Hat-trick Hero** ⚽⚽⚽
- Trigger: Single player scores 3+ goals
- Example: "Ronaldo's Hat-trick Crushes Barcelona!"
- Requires: Player-level goal tracking in match stats

#### 3. **Clean Sheet Master** 🧤
- Trigger: Goalkeeper/team keeps 5th consecutive clean sheet
- Example: "Arsenal's Defense Unstoppable - 5 Clean Sheets in a Row!"
- Context: Track defensive records across matches

#### 4. **Unbeaten Streak** 📈
- Trigger: Team extends unbeaten run to 5, 10, 15 matches
- Example: "Man United Unbeaten in 10 - Can Anyone Stop Them?"
- Context: Calculate win/draw streak

#### 5. **Losing Streak** 📉
- Trigger: Team loses 3rd, 5th consecutive match
- Example: "Crisis at Chelsea - Fifth Straight Defeat!"
- Context: Calculate consecutive losses
- Tone: Harsh or dramatic

#### 6. **Derby/Rivalry Match** ⚔️
- Trigger: Specific team matchups (can be configured)
- Example: "El Clásico Showdown: Real Madrid vs Barcelona!"
- Context: Pre-configured rivalry pairs

#### 7. **Last-Minute Drama** ⏱️
- Trigger: Winning goal scored in 85th minute or later
- Example: "90th Minute Heartbreak for Arsenal!"
- Requires: Goal timing data

#### 8. **Goal Fest vs Previous Encounter** 🎯
- Trigger: Total goals significantly higher than last meeting
- Example: "From 1-0 to 5-4! Managers Trade Blows in Goal Bonanza"
- Context: Compare with previous H2H result

## Tone Distribution Suggestions

- **Neutral**: Record updates, milestones, position changes
- **Dramatic**: Close matches, comebacks, last-minute goals, title races
- **Funny**: Boring draws, winless droughts, mediocrity, manager quotes

---

## Dynamic Tone Selection

The system could automatically choose tone based on:
- Context (crisis = harsh, excitement = dramatic)
- Team position (top teams = neutral/dramatic, struggling = harsh)
- Event type (records = neutral, upsets = dramatic)
- Randomization for variety
