## Position Categories

### 🟡 Goalkeeper (1 position)
- **GK** - The last line of defense

### 🔵 Defenders (3 positions)
- **CB** - Center Back (central defenders)
- **LB** - Left Back (left-sided defenders)
- **RB** - Right Back (right-sided defenders)

### 🟢 Midfielders (5 positions)
- **DMF** - Defensive Midfielder (shields defense)
- **CMF** - Central Midfielder (box-to-box)
- **LMF** - Left Midfielder (wide left)
- **RMF** - Right Midfielder (wide right)
- **AMF** - Attacking Midfielder (creative playmaker)

### 🔴 Forwards (4 positions)
- **SS** - Second Striker (support forward)
- **LWF** - Left Wing Forward (left attacker)
- **RWF** - Right Wing Forward (right attacker)
- **CF** - Center Forward (main striker)

## Filtering Players

Use the position filter to find specific players:

1. **By Category:**
   - All Goalkeepers: Select "GK"
   - All Defenders: Select CB, LB, or RB
   - All Midfielders: Select DMF, CMF, LMF, RMF, or AMF
   - All Forwards: Select SS, LWF, RWF, or CF

2. **By Role:**
   - Central players: GK, CB, DMF, CMF, AMF, SS, CF
   - Wide players: LB, RB, LMF, RMF, LWF, RWF

3. **By Playing Style:**
   - Defensive: GK, CB, LB, RB, DMF
   - Balanced: CMF, LMF, RMF
   - Attacking: AMF, SS, LWF, RWF, CF

## Tips for Team Building

1. **Balanced Squad:** Aim for 2-3 players per position
2. **Priority Positions:** GK, CB, CMF, CF are essential
3. **Versatility:** Players who can play multiple positions add flexibility
4. **Formation Fit:** Consider your preferred formation when bidding
5. **Budget Allocation:** 
   - Premium: CF, AMF, CB (typically higher rated)
   - Value: LB, RB, DMF (often undervalued)
   - Essential: GK (don't skimp on quality)

## FAQ

**Q: What happened to DEF, MID, FWD?**
A: The system was updated to use specific positions for better accuracy and team management.

**Q: Can I still see old position data?**
A: Yes, old positions will display but won't have specific color coding.

**Q: How do I update existing player positions?**
A: Contact your admin to manually update positions in the database, or re-import player data.

**Q: What if a position isn't listed?**
A: The system defaults to CMF for unknown positions. Contact support if you need additional positions.

**Q: Can players play multiple positions?**
A: Currently, each player has one primary position. Future updates may add secondary positions.
