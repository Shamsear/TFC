# Star Rating Feature

## Overview
The star rating system provides a quick visual indicator of player quality using a 1-5 star scale. This complements the numerical overall rating and makes it easier to identify top-tier players at a glance.

## Star Rating Scale

### ⭐ 1 Star (Poor)
- Overall Rating: Below 70
- Description: Below average players, typically reserves or youth players

### ⭐⭐ 2 Stars (Average)
- Overall Rating: 70-74
- Description: Average players, decent squad depth

### ⭐⭐⭐ 3 Stars (Good)
- Overall Rating: 75-79
- Description: Good players, reliable starters
